The Evros / Meriç River

A Century of Border Design
Forensic Architecture
Project Overview

This project traces a century of border design in the Evros / Meriç River region, revealing how infrastructures, policies, and technologies shape the production of borders and human movement.

It combines investigative research, spatial analysis, and open-source techniques to expose state violence and advocate for human rights.

▱Combining architecture, law, journalism, and the sciences.
◉Using open-source investigations and spatial technologies.
♙Working with multidisciplinary teams and collaborators.

About Forensic Architecture

Forensic Architecture is a multidisciplinary research agency based at Goldsmiths, University of London.

It combines architecture, media analysis, and open-source intelligence to investigate human rights violations.
